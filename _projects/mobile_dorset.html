About

Mobile Dorset is an informal gathering of mobile developers, designers and anyone with a passion for mobile in Dorset.

Mobile Dorset brings together Dorset and the surrounding areas thriving mobile development scene. With meetings every month we attract as many as 45 people to discuss the latest news in mobile or hear talks from experts in the field.

We've also been known to hold special events for major industry conferences such as Google I/O or Apple's WWDC.

Being focused on the local community allows us to show case and highlight some of the amazing talent within our area by giving them the oppourtunity to speak freely and openly with other professionals.
